* Meetings will be held weekly at noon on Fridays in the Discord `#steering-committee-meeting` channel
* Start an .md note from the meeting notes template and list all attendees
* Review Action Items - copy over the previous week's action items and record status/completion updates. Copy any incomplete items to the Action Item list for today's meeting
* Review PEP items per the process outlined
* review sessionize and upcoming meeting topics - ensure we've got next 3 months planned (speaker booked or activity outlined) and brainstorm for more ideas
* review agenda from last week and take action
* Every meeting should end with setting an agenda for the next meeting
